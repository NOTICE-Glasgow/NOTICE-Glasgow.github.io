Abstract submission is open

We invite you to submit an abstract to take part in NOTiCE2o23. Accepted abstracts will be presented at the poster session and there is also the opportunity to participate in the flash presentations. In addition, one of the abstracts submitted will be selected by the Scientific and Organisation Committee to be given as an oral presentation.

Submission of abstracts to take part in NOTiCE2o23 is now open. Please download either the MS Word or preferably the Latex template to compile your abstract and email it to phas-notice@glasgow.ac.uk by the 12th of February 2023.

Deadline: extended deadline 12th of February 2023

When submitting an abstract, kindly indicate if you are happy to potentially also give a flash presentation or a full oral presentation. Once your abstract has been selected, you will be sent a link to register for NOTiCE2o23. The workshop is free of charge and registration includes attendance to all sessions, exhibition, conference material, lunches and coffee breaks. Due to limited availability please submit your contribution as soon as possible to avoid disappointment.

Submit your science image

From photo and illustration to microscopy image, visualisations capture our imagination and bring complex concepts to life. Submit your own image showcasing your team, work, science, technology or research along with a short caption to have it featured in our NOTiCE2o23 conference booklet! The conference booklet will be ciruclated to all participants as PDF document and full credits will be given to you/your team in the picture caption. Whether artistic or scientific- share it with us and the NOTiCE community. All rights to the image remain with you.
