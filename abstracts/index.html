Abstract submission is open

Submission of abstracts to take part in NOTiCE is now open. Please download either the MS Word or preferably the Latex template to compile your abstract and email it to phas-notice@glasgow.ac.uk by the 29th of January 2023.

Deadline: 29th of January 2023

We will select one abstract to be presented as an oral talk. If you would like to be considered to give an oral presentation instead of poster, please indicate so in your email. Additionally, there will be the opportunity to highlight your poster to the plenary in a flash presentation session. If you would like to be considered for this flash session, please indicate so in your email.
