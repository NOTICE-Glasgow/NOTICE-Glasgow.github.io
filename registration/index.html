Registration

The workshop is free of charge and registration includes attendance to all sessions, exhibition, conference material, lunches and coffee breaks. Registration opens on the 22nd of January on our eventbrite page. Due to limited availability please register as soon as possible to avoid disappointment.

Submit an abstract

Deadline 1st of May

We invite you to submit an abstract to take part in the poster session and there is also the opportunity to participat in the flash presentations. In addition, one of the abstracts submitted will be selected by the Scientific and Organisation Committee to be given as an oral presentation. When submitting an abstract for the poster session, please select if you are happy to potentially also give a flash presentation or a full oral presentation. Please use the word template for submissions and email your abstract to notice.glasgow2020_at_gmail.com before the 1st of May. Selected abstracts will be circulated in the conference booklet to participants of NOTICE2020.

Submit your science image

From photo and illustration to microscopy image, visualisations capture our imagination and bring complex concepts to life. Submit your own image showcasing your team, work, science, technology or research along with a short caption to have it featured in our NOTICE2020 conference booklet. The conference booklet will be ciruclated to all participants as PDF document and full credits will be given to you/your team in the picture caption. Whether artistic or scientific- share it with us and the NOTICE community. All rights to the image remain with you.
