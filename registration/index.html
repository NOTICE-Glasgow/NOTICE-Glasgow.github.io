Important dates

  • January 15th: registration opens

Registration opens 15th of January 2020

The workshop is free of charge and registration includes attendance to all sessions, exhibition, conference material, lunches and coffee breaks. The social dinner is not included and can be paid on site. Registration opens on the 15th of January on our eventbrite page. Due to limited availability please register as soon as possible to avoid disappointment.

Submit an abstract

Deadline 15th of June

We invite you to submit an abstract to take part in the poster session and there is also the opportunity to participat in the flash presentations. In addition, one of the abstracts submitted will be selected by the Scientific and Organisation Committee to be given as an oral presentation. When submitting an abstract for the poster session, please select if you are happy to potentially also give a flash presentation or a full oral presentation. Please use the word template for submissions and email your abstract to notice.glasgow2020_at_gmail.com before the 15th of June.
